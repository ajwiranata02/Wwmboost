Safety Guide

How to Bind NetEase Account

Follow these simple steps to link your game account to NetEase for piloting verification.

1

Create a NetEase Account

Go to the official NetEase mail registration page (reg.email.163.com) to register a free email account.

2

Open Game Account Center

Launch Where Winds Meet, open the Account Settings on the login screen or in-game menu.

3

Link Social to NetEase Email

Select "Bind Email" or "Link Account" and enter the credentials of the NetEase account you registered in Step 1.

4

Complete Piloting Verification

Submit your NetEase email login on checkout. Our boosters will request the 2FA code via WhatsApp when logging in.
